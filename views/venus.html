This is Venus! This planet is the second most brightest object in the night sky!

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and is Earth's closest neighbor in the solar system. Venus sometimes looks like a bright star in the morning or evening sky. The planet is a little smaller than Earth, and is similar to Earth inside.
